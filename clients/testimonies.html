Client Testimonies

~ The following testimonies are from past and current clients ~




I met Kacey for the first time when we were both in high school at a small show, at a barn in Ft. Madison, Iowa. I watched her excel in all classes, and I remember being in awe of how well she rode the courses. Throughout the years I’ve watched her progress and successes in the horse industry. And when I finally got my own horse, I knew if he ever needed a little extra help, I’d send him to the person I trusted most, Kacey. Angus, my Tennessee Walker was gifted to me when he was only 2 1/2. I knew he’d be an excellent trail horse, but he just needed to learn every twig snap, or water crossing wasn’t the end of the world. I had to undergo a major spinal injury and surgery the summer after I got him, so I wanted to make sure he was safe for me to blaze the trails the following year. Kacey took him on for about a month and worked with him almost daily to make sure he was the perfect steed on the trails. And she more than exceeded my expectations, he became the best, most trustworthy trail partner a girl could ask for. Even now, at his current owners, he’s still their “go to” trail horse. Not only is Kacey dependable, she’s caring, responsible, and extremely trustworthy. She will forever get my business whenever my horses need some fine tuning, or even breaking.

Emmalea McElhinney & Angus (2020 Client)







I found Kacey on Facebook a couple of years ago - I believe she was just out of high school. She was in the same group about horses I was, I reached out to her and asked her if she would be willing to work with my girl; Amora. I had been watching some of the things she had been doing with her pony Tornado - I was very impressed. I am disabled and it is hard for me to get on and off my girl. Kacey agreed to help me - I hadn’t had Amora very long when I asked Kacey for her help. Amora was leery of people she didn’t know, but not with Kacey. She was comfortable with her. This put me at ease leaving my girl with her. Kacey kept me in the loop on how things were going at all times. Kacey had Amora laying down for you to mount in no time at all. Kacey is very talented, she has a good hand when working with your horse. I highly recommend her to everyone. She may be young but she is good. She has the true heart for the horse and her work shows it. A very satisfied customer.

Faith Thomas & Amora (2020 Client)
