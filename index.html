Welcome to Rocky's Horsemanship


Connection with the horse is the very first step. When working with a horse I feel it's important to not only get to know the horse in front of you but to also let the horse get to know you. This is a step I use with every horse I work with, whether they have never been handled, had a perfect upbringing, or have had a troubled past, this is a step I will never skip.

At Rocky's Horsemanship we offer five different services which all will be tailored to your horse's specific needs based on where they are at currently. The goal with every horse is to give them a solid foundation to continue improving in a positive manner even after going home. To aid you and your horse afterwards - on the day of pickup, I will always demonstrate and explain where your horse is at, a bit of how they got there, and depending on what you brought your horse in for I may even have you do an exercise with them so you are both on the same page together. (Example: You brought your horse in for 30 days to be started, when it came time for pick up your horse walks, jogs, lopes, stops, backs, and turns. I would demonstrate how I ask for the gaits, explain any quirks I noticed, and then let you get on and ride a bit before heading home.)
