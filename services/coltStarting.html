Colt Starting

$800/30 Days

• For every additional 30 days after your horse has reached 90 days with us, the cost becomes $700/30 days. •

Are you wanting to start new adventures with your two-year old? Have a sensitive horse you want to start under saddle but not sure where to start? Wanting someone willing to take the time to learn your horse, and put a gentle start on them? Well you've come to the right place!


At Rocky's Horsemanship, Colt Starting is taken with a very gentle hand to help keep your horse calm, quiet, and thinking instead of reacting. When looking for a trainer to start your horse, you want someone who is going to pay attention to them to know when they are ready to advance to the next step. You want to be able to take them home, to get on, and continue helping them learn and grow with you. That's what we offer here - the basic goal we have for a horse in for 30 days is that they will walk, jog, lope on both leads, stop, and back in a calm, controlled manner all outside of a pen. They won't be perfect, but the goal is that they are calm, trying, and have an idea of what you're after.

We work with a wide range of different breeds/horses, however they must be at least two-years-old or older for us to start them. The minimum duration for a horse to be started with us is 30 days, unless the horse has had very minimal to no handling in which case the minimum stay would be 60 days.

Please note that the price includes board, daily care, feeding 2x a day(Your hay and grain or just hay), and my training fee.
Payment is cash only unless discussed differently - you can do 50% up front and 50% before you pick up your horse, or 100% at drop off. NO CHECKS
