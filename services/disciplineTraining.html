Discipline Training

$800/30 Days

Have your horse riding the trails and want to try barrel racing? Do you have a pole bending horse and now you want to take on some jumps or trail riding? Want to start your horse in multiple disciplines, but not sure where to start? Well you've come to the right place - at Rocky's Horsemanship we have experience with multiple different disciplines from Western to English!


(Video shows me working with a 3-year-old over some obstacles who came in for 30 days to be started)



While most of my experience is in Western riding, I do have experience riding/training in English as well. I can train for disciplines such as jumping, ranch pleasure, barrel racing, trail work, halter, slowing down for western pleasure, showmanship, showing trail(opening gates, dragging items, picking things up, etc.), and a few other disciplines as well - just ask!

When your horse is here, wether they are in to be trained for jumping, western pleasure, halter, etc. if there is something particular you would like added/worked on(side-passing, backing up, turning on the forehand/hindquarters, standing still, etc.) please feel free to bring it up when we are discussing opportunities! Horses are expected to stay for a minimum of 30 days, however there is not a limit on how long their stay can be.

Please note that the price includes board, daily care, feeding 2x a day (Your hay and grain or just hay), and my training fee.
Payment is cash only unless discussed differently - you can do 50% up front and 50% before you pick up your horse, or 100% at drop off.
NO CHECKS
