Fundamental Groundwork

$200/7 Days

Do you have a young one you'd like to start working with, but not sure how? Have an unhandled horse you want an extra hand with? Maybe even one you've put lots of time on and just want some extra work done before you get on? Well you've come to the right place!


At Rocky's Horsemanship the Fundamental Groundwork is one of the most important steps in getting your horse started on the right hoof. For this service you can pick a minimum of one week(7 days) with no maximum training length. The only age limit for this service is that your horse must be at least one-year-old so they can be stalled on their own. In the Fundamental Groundwork we cover a range of things from halter breaking, teaching them to lead and tie, pick up their feet, bathing, and trailer loading. Depending on their age we can also start putting pads and saddles on them(If you are after this service please note that we will not mount, ride, train for the bit, etc. if that is what you are after, please see the 'Colt Starting' tab). If you would like to start with the Fundamental Groundwork and move into the Colt Starting, or something similar, please reach out and we can start talking about possibilities!

Please note that the price includes board, daily care, feeding 2x a day(Your hay and grain or just hay), and my training fee.
Payment is cash only unless discussed differently - you can do 50% up front and 50% before you pick up your horse, or 100% at drop off.
