Refreshers

$400/14 Days

Has your horse been sitting for a few months maybe even a year or two but they were riding before? Are you wanting to sell your horse and want to see what they remember before you list them? Rode your horse a few years ago, got busy, and want to ride again but not sure what they remember? Well, you're at the right place then!



At Rocky's Horsemanship we will gladly put some time on your horse(s) to see what they still remember and what could be worked on. You are welcome to leave them with us for a minimum of 14 days/two weeks with no maximum stay. You are able to turn the refresher into discipline training if desired. For example, you bring your horse in for three weeks to be refreshed, and then you want to go into jumping or showing trail for 30 days. We would discuss where your horse is at for refreshing, and then come up with a training plan that would work for both of you.

When inquiring about a refresher for your horse(s) please include what you believe your horse has already done, or was doing with you before they had some time off. This will be very helpful for us to create a training plan that's suitable for both you and your horse(s) to give you both the best outcome from their stay!


Please note that the price includes board, daily care, feeding 2x a day(Your hay and grain or just hay), and my training fee.
Payment is cash only unless discussed differently - you can do 50% up front and 50% before you pick up your horse, or 100% at drop off.
NO CHECKS
